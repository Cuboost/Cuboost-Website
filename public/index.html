About Cuboost

Cuboost was made for fun. It first all started with a game: "A cube that can boost!".

Then, it evolved into something more than that: a helpful website that can help with many things. You can flip a coin or check the time with my clock... It's all in one place!

You might have noticed, this website contains no ads and I do not collect anything you type, play, or create; so do not worry about your privacy: what happens on this website, stays private only to you.
